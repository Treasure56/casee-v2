# 📱 Casee — Custom Phone Case Designer

**Casee** is a web application that lets users design and order custom phone cases using their own images. Upload a photo, choose your phone model, pick materials, colors, and finishes — then preview your one-of-a-kind case and checkout.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Image Upload** — Drag-and-drop or click to upload your own image (JPG, PNG, JPEG)
- **Phone Model Selection** — Choose from iPhone 14, 14 Pro, 15, 15 Pro, and 15 Pro Max
- **Case Customization** — Pick from 6 case colors, 2 materials (Silicone / Polycarbonate), and 2 finishes (Smooth / Textured)
- **Live Preview** — See your custom case rendered on a realistic phone mockup
- **Dynamic Pricing** — Price updates in real-time based on your material and finish selections
- **Order Summary & Checkout** — Review your configuration with a full breakdown before purchasing
- **Dark / Light Theme** — System-aware theme toggle powered by `next-themes`
- **Authentication** — Login, Register, and Forgot Password flows
- **Responsive Design** — Fully responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Smooth Animations** — GSAP and Framer Motion powered animations throughout
